Redeem your points for e-vouchers
Al Mashreq points are accumulated upon using any of the bank services such as money withdrawals and using credit cards, and can be redeemed for e-vouchers.
1
Check available partners on Mashreq Rewards, and choose one you would like to shop at.
2
Check available e-vouchers on Mashreq Rewards homepage
3
Click on one of the e-vouchers to purchase using your points.
4
Use your e-voucher code at your desired partner branch for discount.
